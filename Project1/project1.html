TRANSPORTATION

Choose a transportation method that you like

  • Airlines

    There are nine international airports in Vietnam: Hanoi Nei Pai International Airport (HAN), Haiphong Jibei International Airport (HPH), Hue Fu International Airport, Da Nang International Airport (DAD), Nha Trang Jinlan International Airport (CXR), and Lilian Jiang International Airport (DLI), Ho Chi Minh City, Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN), Can Tho Airport (VCA), Phu Quoc Airport Phu Quoc Airport (PQC).

  • Train

    Vietnam is a long and narrow country, and not all cities can travel by train. The only common train line running through the north and south is the first line of Hanoi-Ho Chi Minh City. It passes through important cities such as Hue and Da Nang, and there is basically only one train at the same time. Ho Chi Minh City has another Ho Chi Minh-Phan Thiet City (Minna) line, and there are Hanoi-Lao Street, Hanoi-Nanning, etc. to the north.

  • Bus

    The most common way to travel in Vietnam is Open Bus. The time is more free to choose. Usually you can buy tickets 1-2 days in advance. Please choose a regular company, foreign tourists will take the car of Sin Tourist (new coffee), FUTA BUS and other companies.
